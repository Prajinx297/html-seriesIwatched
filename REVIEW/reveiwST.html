STRANGER THINGS

ABOUT

In 1980s Indiana, a group of young friends witness supernatural forces and secret government exploits. As they search for answers, the children unravel a series of extraordinary mysteries.

GENRE

  • Science Fiction
  • Horror
  • Mystery
  • Drama

REVEIWS

  • IMDb: The show has an overall rating of 8.4/10 based on over 4,895 user ratings. Many users praised the show for its nostalgic '80s atmosphere, engaging story, and well-developed characters.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: The first season has a 4.5/5 rating from critics and a 90% audience score. The show is often praised for its suspense, character development, and homage to '80s pop culture.
  • Metacritic: The user score is 8.4/10, indicating generally favorable reviews. Critics have highlighted the show's strong storytelling, character arcs, and the effective blend of horror and sci-fi elements.
    • Overall, "Stranger Things" has been praised for its compelling story, nostalgic references, and strong performances, making it a favorite among both critics and audiences.
