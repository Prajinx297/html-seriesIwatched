THE IDOL

ABOUT

After a nervous breakdown, Jocelyn, an aspiring model, resumes on her journey to become the greatest pop star in the USA and then, she meets Tedros, a contemporary cult's head.

GENRE

  • Drama

REVEIWS

  • IMDb: The show has an overall rating of 5.9/10. While the performances, particularly by Lily-Rose Depp and Abel "The Weeknd" Tesfaye, are praised, some users found the plot execution underwhelming and the pacing slow
  • Rotten Tomatoes: "The Idol" holds a 63% rating from critics and a 3.5/5 rating from the audience. Critics appreciated the concept and cast but were divided on the show's storytelling and tension
  • Metacritic: The series received a score of 63, indicating generally favorable reviews. Critics praised the performances and concept but felt the show didn't fully deliver on its spy thriller potential
    • Overall, "The Idol" has mixed reviews, with notable praise for its cast and concept but some disappointment in its execution.
