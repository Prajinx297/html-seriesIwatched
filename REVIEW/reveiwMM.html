MS.MARVEL

ABOUT

Kamala is a superhero fan with an imagination, particularly when it comes to Captain Marvel; Kamala feels like she doesn't fit in at school and sometimes even at home, that is until she gets superpowers like the heroes she's looked up to.

GENRE

  • Action-Adventure
  • Comedy
  • Coming of age
  • Superhero

REVEIWS

  • IMDb:The show has an overall rating of 6.2/10. Users appreciated the fresh take on the MCU and Iman Vellani's performance as Kamala Khan, but some felt the series had pacing issues and filler scenes.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: The series holds a 9/10 rating from critics and a 4/5 rating from the audience. It's praised for its engaging story, cultural representation, and strong performances, though some critics noted weaknesses in the villain portrayal.
  • Metacritic: The show has received a score of 87, indicating generally favorable reviews. The show is celebrated for its innovative storytelling, cultural authenticity, and Iman Vellani's charismatic performance.
    • Overall, "Ms. Marvel" has been well-received for its fresh perspective and strong performances, though it has faced some criticism for pacing and certain plot elements.
