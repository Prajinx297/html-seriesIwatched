REVEIWS

  • IMDb: Users have given the show a rating of 6.6/10. Many reviewers appreciate the unique concept, character development, and visual effects. However, some have mentioned that the show can be predictable and melodramatic
  • Rotten Tomatoes: The show has a 61 Metascore based on 26 critic reviews. While some reviewers have praised its fantasy elements, production design, and emotional depth, others have noted that it can feel superficial at times.
  • Metacritic: The show has a score of 61 based on 226 user ratings. Reviews often highlight the engaging storyline, diverse cast, and fantasy elements. However, there are mentions of the show being slow-paced and overly reliant on teenage drama.
    • Overall, Locke & Key seems to be a hit with fans of fantasy and supernatural genres, but it may not be for everyone.
