SHE HULK:ATTORNEY AT LAW

ABOUT

She-Hulk: Attorney at Law is an American television miniseries created by Jessica Gao for the streaming service Disney+, based on Marvel Comics featuring the character She-Hulk

GENRE

  • Superhero
  • Comedy
  • Legal Drama

REVEIWS

  • IMDb:"She-Hulk: Attorney at Law" has an overall rating of 6.2/10. Users appreciated the performances by Tatiana Maslany and the meta-comedy elements, but criticized the CGI for She-Hulk and felt the series lacked focus on legal aspects
  • Rotten Tomatoes: The series holds a 74% rating from critics and a 3.5/5 rating from the audience. Critics praised the show's fresh take on the MCU and Maslany's performance, but some felt the series had pacing issues and an overabundance of cameos.
  • Metacritic:"She-Hulk: Attorney at Law" received a score of 63, indicating generally favorable reviews. The show was noted for its humor and strong performances, though some critics felt it didn't fully deliver on its potential.
    • Overall, "She-Hulk: Attorney at Law" has received mixed reviews, with praise for its performances and humor, but criticism for its CGI and pacing.
