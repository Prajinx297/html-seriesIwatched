SECRET INVASION

ABOUT

Nick Fury learns of a secret invasion of Earth by a faction of shapeshifting Skrulls; Fury joins his allies, and together they race against time to thwart an imminent Skrull invasion and save humanity.

GENRE

  • Action Adventure
  • Suspense
  • Drama
  • Superhero
  • Mystery

REVEIWS

  • IMDb: The show has an overall rating of 5.9/10. While the performances, particularly by Samuel L. Jackson, are praised, some users found the plot execution underwhelming and the pacing slow.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: "Secret Invasion" holds a 63% rating from critics and a 3.5/5 rating from the audience. The darker tone and unique take on the MCU were appreciated, but the series faced criticism for its lack of tension and excitement.
  • Metacritic: The series received a score of 63, indicating generally favorable reviews. Critics praised the performances and concept but felt the show didn't fully deliver on its spy thriller potential.
    • Overall, "Secret Invasion" has mixed reviews, with notable praise for its cast and concept but some disappointment in its execution.
