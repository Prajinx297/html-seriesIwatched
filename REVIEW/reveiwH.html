HAWKEYE

ABOUT

REVEIWS

  • IMDb:The show has an overall rating of 7.5/10. Users appreciated the performances by Jeremy Renner and Hailee Steinfeld, as well as the holiday-themed storyline and the introduction of new characters like Kate Bishop1. However, some felt the series could have been more focused and had pacing issues.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: The series holds a 74% rating from critics and a 3.5/5 rating from the audience. Critics praised the chemistry between Renner and Steinfeld, the holiday humor, and the street-level action2. However, some felt the plot was unfocused and the series missed the mark at times.
  • Metacritic: The show has received a score of 74, indicating generally favorable reviews. The show was noted for its strong performances and fresh take on the MCU, though some critics felt it could have been more engaging and better developed.
    • Overall, "Hawkeye" is well-regarded for its performances and holiday cheer, but it has faced some criticism for its pacing and plot execution.
