ABOUT

Nine noble families wage war against each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros. Meanwhile, a force is rising after millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.

REVEIWS

  • IMDb: Many users praised the show for its suspense, character development, and plot twists, especially in the first six seasons. It was often described as one of the best TV series ever made
  • Rotten Tomatoes: Critics and audiences alike appreciated the show's adaptation of George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series, with high ratings for its storytelling, production values, and performances.
  • Metacritic: The show received "universal acclaim" from critics, with high scores for its first six seasons. The series was praised for its pacing, acting, and visual effects
    • Overall, "Game of Thrones" is still considered a landmark in television, despite the mixed feelings about its conclusion.
