Wanda Vision

ABOUT

Vision and Wanda live a normal life in Westview and conceal their superpowers. However, as decades pass by, they start doubting that everything is not what it seems.

GENRE

  • Superhero
  • Comedy

REVEIWS

  • IGN: The show has been described as bold and experimental, blending surreal sitcom, puzzle box mystery, and superhero dramatics. The themes of grief and love between Wanda and Vision are often mentioned as strong points
  • Rotten Tomatoes: The show has a 93% rating from critics and a 4.5/5 rating from audiences. Reviewers have highlighted its unique take on the MCU, charismatic performances by Elizabeth Olsen and Paul Bettany, and visually stunning effects. Some have noted that it's a delightful diversion from the usual MCU fare.
  • CNET: Reviewers have called it weird, funny, and laden with Easter eggs, praising it as an experimental win for Marvel TV
