ALICE IN BORDERLAND

ABOUT

Obsessed gamer Arisu suddenly finds himself in a strange, emptied-out version of Tokyo in which he and his friends must compete in dangerous games in order to survive.

GENRE

  • Drama
  • Thrller
  • Science Fiction

REVEIWS

  • IMDb: Users have given the show a rating of 7.7/10. Reviews mention the visually stunning scenes and well-executed action sequences. Some users have pointed out plot inconsistencies and luck-based game outcomes.
  • Rotten Tomatoes: The show has received praise for its gripping storyline and memorable characters. Some reviewers have highlighted the cinematic quality and intense survival games. However, there are mentions of plot holes and unresolved questions.
  • Metacritic: The show has a score of 83 from critics and 7.7 from users. The reviews often mention the emotional depth and character development, but also note the violent and sadistic elements.
    • Overall, it seems like Alice in Borderland has a mix of strong points and areas for improvement. If you enjoy high-stakes survival dramas with a mix of action and emotion, it might be worth a watch!
